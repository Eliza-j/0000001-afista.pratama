China Law & Reference

China Law & Reference is a legal information query tool specially designed for the Chinese market by Wolters Kluwer. China HR Reference

China HR Reference is a labor law information query tool specially designed for the Chinese market by Wolters Kluwer. It covers a wide range of contents including bilingual legislations, case analysis, training, commentaries, Q&A and smart tools, etc. which can help HR professionals handle employment relations and prevent/control employment risks.

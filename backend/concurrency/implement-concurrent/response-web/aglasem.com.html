Ed-Tech Solutions

Test From Home - Online Proctored Exams

The latest ed-tech solution by Aglasem is Test From Home (TFH).

TFH is an AI-ML based online proctored exam platform that enables educational institutions to hold completely online exams. Now schools and colleges can conduct entrance exams, internal exams without compromising on the safety of students and teachers!

USPs of TFH

  • Exam is completely online and center-less.
  • AI and ML technology has been used to make the online proctored intuitively cheating free.
  • Audio, video monitoring, and facial recognition and facial features study to catch attempts of cheating
  • Can be deployed in one week.
  • Cost-effective.

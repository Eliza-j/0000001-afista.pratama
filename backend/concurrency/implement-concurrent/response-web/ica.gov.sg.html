MyICA
Scheduled Maintenance

MyICA and ICA e-Services will not be available on 01 May 2022, 0001hrs to 0800hrs (Singapore Time) for scheduled maintenance.

Please plan your transactions in advance. We apologise for any inconvenience caused.
Longer Processing Time for Passport Applications

ICA has received high number of passport applications. We seek your understanding that more time will be required to process your passport applications. 

Applicants can refer to the e-Service to check the status of your passport application.

Eligible applicants can make an appointment to collect the passports at designated SingPost offices without paying any additional collection fee.

Refer to Passport Collection at SingPost for more information.
Vaccination Requirement for the grant of new Permanent Residence, Long-Term Visit Pass and Student's Pass

From 1 February 2022, COVID-19 vaccination will be a condition for the grant of new Permanent Residence (PR), Long-Term Visit Pass (LTVP) and Student’s Pass (STP) applications.

Refer to the Press Release for more information.
Services at ICA Building in view of COVID-19 Counter services at the ICA Building are available for selected services or by appointment only.

Members of the public should continue to use ICA's e-Services available at MyICA.

Click the link for more information about services at ICA Building.

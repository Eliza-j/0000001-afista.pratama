the Paymentus Instant Payment Network®

MORE WAYS TO PAY AND GET PAID WITH THE INSTANT PAYMENT NETWORK®

Our Instant Payment Network (IPN) extends the reach of our platform, giving businesses more ways to connect to their customers through their preferred channels, all with a single integration to Paymentus.

IPN makes it possible for consumers to manage and pay bills through Amazon Alexa, inside the PayPal app, at businesses like Walmart and via online banking for hundreds of banks.

Millions of consumers use the Paymentus platform to pay their bills each month. Our strategic partners help make it happen. Together, we simplify how bills get paid.

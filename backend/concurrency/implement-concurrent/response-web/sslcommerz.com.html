      Security is in Our DNA

      We have invested heavily in industry-standard infrastructure to ensure that your customer's data safe and secured.

      PCI DSS & ISO:27001 Compliant

      We have the best-in-class security protocols and certifications in place so that you don’t have to worry about security. We have you covered.

      Secure & Encrypted Communication

      We use the highest assurance SSL/TLS certificate, which makes sure that no unauthorized person can access your sensitive payment data over the internet.

      Tokenization to prevent Data Theft

      We replace your 16-digit card number with a token, which replaces the original card number. Tokens are assigned randomly, making it extremely difficult to crack.

      We are Serious about Compliance

      SSLCOMMERZ has PCI-DSS certificates, 3D security systems ISO 27001 compliance and anti-fraud systems. We use industry-standard EV SSL protocol. We have a dedicated fraud control team and independent data centres which minimizes the risk of failure, guarantees business continuity and security for all the user data.

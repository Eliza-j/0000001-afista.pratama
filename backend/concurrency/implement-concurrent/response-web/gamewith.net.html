Editor's Choice

Genshin Impact

Genshin Impact is a free to play RPG game created by miHoYo. Explore the open-world map freely as a traveler from another world and go on a journey to reunite with your long-lost sibling.

Players can choose to play solo and experience an immersive single-player campaign or invite friends to join the fight against dangerous monsters with 4-player cross-play for PS4, iOS, Android, and PC.

Genshin Impact Wiki Guide

Pokemon Legends: Arceus

Pokemon Legends: Arceus is a semi-open world addition to the series loved by most, Pokemon. Explore the land of Hisui as you capture different Pokemon new and old. As you go on the game, you will learn about the Legendary Pokemon called Arceus and the past of the Sinnoh Region. This game is available for Nintendo Switch.

Pokemon Legends: Arceus Guide

Pokemon Unite

Pokemon Unite is a battle arena multiplayer Pokemon game. Play as the Pokemon itself and battle against the opponents to collect more points. The game is available for Android, iOS and Nintendo Switch.

Pokemon Unite Guide

MIR4

MIR4 is a free to play MMORPG which also features play to earn mechanics and full of PVP action including large scale clan wars. The game also support cross-platform gaming with PC & Mobile (Android & iOS).

MIR4 Wiki Guide

Axie Infinity

Axie Infinity is a game universe where you can collect, breed, and battle virtual Monsters called Axie. Build your own nation of Axies in this game available for Android, iOs, & PC (Windows & Mac).

Axie Infinity Guide

Lost Ark

Lost Ark is a free-to-play MMO Action RPG that features a massive world of 2.5D Fantasy. Use different classes and skills as you search for the Lost Ark that will be used to fight against evil. This game is available on Microsoft Windows PC.

Lost Ark Wiki Guide

Elden Ring

Play the hottest Dark Fantasy open-world game Elden Ring! Explore the world and learn about the powerful item called "Elden Ring" and battle with challenging enemies. The game is available on Microsoft Windows PC and PS Consoles (PS4 and PS5)

Frequently asked questions

  • What is Snov.io?

    Snov.io is an outreach automation platform for marketers and sales offering a variety of tools for lead generation, email verification, email sending, and email tracking.

  • How does the free plan work?

    We offer a forever free, renewable plan with 50 monthly credits and 100 drip campaign recipients for you to try out all the tools and features. If you need to go over this limit, you can purchase a plan that best suits your needs.

  • What are credits and recipients?

    Credit is a payment unit you can spend on the platform to use Email Verifier and Email Finder tools. Email recipients are used in the Email Drip Campaigns tool. You can discover what 1 credit can buy you here. 1 recipient stands for 1 contacted person (regardless of the number of emails sent to that person).

  • Can I use all the tools on any plan?

    All our tools (Email Finder, Email Verifier, Drip Campaigns, and extensions) are unlocked on all plans. Integrations, data export and bulk features are available on all paid plans.

  • Will I receive all the features described above?

    Yes. Paid accounts have access to all the features described above, and free accounts are only limited in integrations and data export.

  • What types of payment do you accept?

    All payments are processed by a third-party payment processing service. It currently offers the following options:

    - Visa

    - MasterCard

    - Discover

    - American Express

    - Diners

    - JCB

    - PIN debit cards with Visa or MasterCard

    - debit cards with Visa or MasterCard

    - PayPal

    and others depending on your country.

  • Do you offer demos?

    Yes. You can book a demo with our Customer Success Agent for yourself or your team.

  • What is your refund policy?

    To request a refund, please contact our team via the Support Chat.

  • I have more questions.

    If you have any questions, you can check our Knowledgebase, contact our Customer Care team via the Support chat, or email us at help@snov.io.

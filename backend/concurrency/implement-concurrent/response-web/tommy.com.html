    TOMMY HILFIGER

    Iconically cool and preppy styles, refreshed for the now.

    As the pioneer of prep, Tommy is reimagining this classic style that’s currently captivating the fashion world. We’re uplifting a new generation and tossing aside gender and cultural customs, and creating a new preppy code. ‘Prep, for all’ is the mantra that’s woven into this season’s collection.

    PREP IS BACK

    Spring is nature's way of hitting refresh. Inspired by the season, we've given new life to our forever iconic, preppy style. All-American and classically cool, this season's collection borrows from the archives, but has been modernized for the new era.

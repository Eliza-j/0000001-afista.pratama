Who is Dynata?

Dynata is a company which helps researchers listen to and access people's opinions. Dynata is a member of the Council of American Survey Research Organizations (www.casro.org) and complies with research industry standards, best practices and relevant laws. Dynata believes that legitimate research is a vital part of society because when people give their opinions, they can help shape, improve, and create products, services, programs, and policies that impact people, society and the world we live in.

Pokémon Sword & Shield

Battles for the latest online competition have begun. This competition, the 2022 International Challenge April is a Double Battle competition with standard VGC rules. All players who enter and participate in at least 3 battles will receive a Shiny Galarian Moltres
Battles will run from April 15th 00:00 UTC to April 17th 23:59 UTC.
We have full details of this competition, including an eligible Pokémon list, in our 2022 International Challenge April section

Pokémon TCG - Dark Phantasma

The next Pokémon TCG set has been revealed for Japan: Dark Phantasma. This set is due for release on May 13th and focuses around Hisuian Zoroark with Hisuian Goodra and Shiny Gardevoir cards

Pokémon GO

Niantic have announced the next Pokémon GO event. This event is the Sustainability Week event and is due to run from April 20th at 10:00 to April 25th at 20:00 local time and introduces Oranguru and Shiny Cherubi into the game
There will be Timed Research during Community dsay on April 23rd requiring you to walk 1km per stage
A collection Challenge will run that will earn a Mossy Lure, Great Balls. You need to use a Mossy Lure to get Weepinbell and Gloom
Venusaur, Oddish, Grimey, Turtwig, Grotle, Cherubi, Trubbish,Ferroseed, Phantump and Oranguru will spawn in the wild
Alolan Diglett, Larvitar, Cherubi and Oranguru will be in 7km Egggs
Gloom, Weepinbell, Chikorita, Grotle, Cherubi, Sewaddle, Alolan Diglett, Bellossom and Nincada will be in Mossy Lure Modules
Turtwig, Cherubi, Snivy, Trubbish, Chespin and Phantump will be in Field Research

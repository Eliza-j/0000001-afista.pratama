How we score VPN services

Support

No matter how good a VPN service is, it is important to have a way to contact customer support. Whether you've run into technical, billing or other issues, multiple support options is a must have for a good VPN provider.

Features

A variety of features is what makes a VPN great. Bandwidth limits, kill-switches, server numbers, simultaneous connections - we go through each and every one of them so you are provided with the most relevant information about a VPN service.

Pricing

We present the pricing plans and their differences, go over the acceptable payment methods as well as refund policies. We will definitely tell you if a service is worth its price or if it might be too much for what it offers.

Reliable

A VPN must be reliable, hands down. If a VPN provider is not reliable, you and your Internet activity will never be fully protected. We review the privacy policy, encryption protocols and their strength as well as how reliable are they.

Speed

If you're using a VPN for streaming services, Torrenting or other activity - speed is of the essence. Your Internet speed is cut down when using VPN in general, so it's important that it offers high-speed servers. That way, your connection will stay relatively similar to what it's like without using VPN services.

Platforms

Simultaneous connections and numerous device compatibility is important. You may want to use a VPN on you tablet, your phone and your computer all at the same time, not to mention that the VPN must be compatible with different operating systems - from Windows to Linux.
